01 — Why We Exist

We started because
we saw a gap.

Most companies hiring in BFSI, IT, or Pharma face the same problem: too many CVs that don't fit, or too long a wait for the ones that do. We built Eureka to fix both.

01
Most agencies send volume. You need quality.
Sending twenty CVs and hoping the client filters them is an industry habit that wastes your time. We send two or three candidates we would genuinely vouch for — each spoken to, assessed, and specifically matched to your brief. Not a pile. A shortlist.
02
Technology without judgment is just noise.
AI can scan thousands of CVs. But it cannot tell you that a candidate will accept a counter-offer, or that their "8 years of experience" is really 3 years repeated. That call is human. We use AI where it genuinely helps — speed and sourcing — and people where it actually matters: every decision, every placement.
02 — How AI Fits In

AI makes us faster.
Humans make us right.

We are honest about what AI can and cannot do. It accelerates sourcing and data. It does not replace the judgment that makes a placement stick.

Where we use AI Makes us faster
Scanning and ranking thousands of candidate profiles across Naukri, LinkedIn, and GitHub — in hours, not days.
Generating role-specific boolean search strings that surface candidates generalist searches miss entirely.
Scoring CVs against your specific requirements before any human time is spent reviewing them.
Compiling accurate, up-to-date salary benchmarks and market intelligence to inform your hiring decisions.
Where humans decide Makes us right
Every screening call. A CV score cannot hear hesitation, probe a vague answer, or assess genuine motivation.
The final shortlist decision. We only send candidates we would personally recommend — AI narrows the field, we make the call.
Offer negotiation and candidate management. Counter-offers, cold feet, last-minute concerns — these are human conversations.
Every client relationship. The trust that earns a repeat mandate is built person to person — not algorithm to algorithm.

04 — How We Work

From brief to joining.
What actually happens.

Six steps. No black boxes. Each one reflects a deliberate choice about how to do this better than most agencies do.

1
Discovery & Intake Call — not just the JD
Before we start any search, we ask what the last rejected candidates lacked, what success looks like at six months, and what the real urgency is. A well-run intake call replaces three weeks of blind sourcing. The person you brief is the same person who runs your entire search — no handoffs to juniors.
Human — always
2
AI-Powered Sourcing
We run multi-platform searches across Naukri Resdex, LinkedIn, GitHub, and niche communities using AI-generated search strings tailored to your specific requirement — producing a large, relevant candidate pool quickly after mandate signing.
AI-powered
3
Personal Screening — every candidate, every time
Every candidate who clears our AI filter gets a structured phone call. We verify skills, understand real motivation, assess counter-offer risk, and confirm actual CTC expectations and availability. The profile you receive reflects what we heard on the call — not what someone wrote on their CV seven months ago.
Human — always
4
Curated Shortlist — 2 to 3 Candidates
We send two or three candidates — never more. The discipline of shortlisting forces quality and protects your time from the noise that most agencies send by default.
AI-assisted, human-approved
5
Interview Coordination & Offer Management
We brief candidates before every interview. We manage the offer negotiation and handle counter-offer conversations. We keep the process moving so strong candidates don't accept other offers while yours pauses between rounds.
Human — always
6
90-Day Replacement Guarantee
If the placement does not work out within 90 days for any fit-related reason, we replace at no additional cost.
Guaranteed
